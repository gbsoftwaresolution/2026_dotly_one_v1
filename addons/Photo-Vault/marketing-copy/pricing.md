## Payment Options

You can pay with cryptocurrency or a credit card.

Crypto payments are processed through a payment provider. We accept Bitcoin, Ethereum, and other major cryptocurrencies.

Credit card payments are processed through Stripe. We do not store your card information. Stripe handles all payment data.

---

## No Auto-Renewal

We do not automatically renew your subscription.

When your subscription period ends, you will receive a notification. You can choose to renew manually. If you do not renew, your account switches to read-only mode.

In read-only mode, you can log in, view your photos, and download your files. You cannot upload new photos. Your existing data remains encrypted and accessible.

---

## Refund Policy

If you are not satisfied within the first 30 days, we will refund your payment.

Email us with your account email address. We will process the refund within 5 business days.

After 30 days, we do not offer refunds for unused time. This is because our costs for storage and infrastructure are fixed.

---

## What Happens If You Stop Paying

Your data is not deleted.

If your subscription expires and you do not renew, your account becomes read-only. You can log in. You can view your files. You can download everything. You cannot upload new files.

Your data remains on our servers for 90 days after expiration. During this time, you can renew and regain full access.

After 90 days, we may delete your account and encrypted files to free up storage. We will email you before this happens.

## Trust & Transparency

There are no hidden fees. The price you see is the price you pay.

We do not charge extra for bandwidth. We do not charge for downloads or exports. We do not charge per user or per device.

If prices change in the future, your current subscription period is honored at the original price. You are notified before renewal if the price has changed.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?**
You can purchase a new plan at any time. The new plan starts immediately and the old plan is credited as a prorated refund.

**Do you offer discounts for nonprofits or students?**
Not at this time. Our pricing is already structured to be affordable.

**What if I need more storage?**
All plans include unlimited storage. There are no storage limits.

**Can I share my account with family members?**
Each subscription is for one user. We do not currently offer family plans.

**What if my payment fails?**
We will notify you by email. You have 7 days to update your payment method before your account switches to read-only mode.
