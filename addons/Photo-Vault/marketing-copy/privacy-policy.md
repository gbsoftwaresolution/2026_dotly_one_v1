# BoosterAi.me — Privacy Policy

---

## Page Headline
We cannot see your photos. Here is what we know.

## Sub-headline
BoosterAi.me uses zero-knowledge encryption. We store encrypted files we cannot read. This privacy policy explains what data we do collect and why.

## Last Updated
February 2026

---

## What We Cannot See

We cannot see your photos or videos. They are encrypted on your device before upload.

We cannot see your encryption keys. They never leave your device.

We cannot decrypt your files. We do not have the technical capability to do this.

This is not a choice we made. It is how the system is built.

---

## What We Do Collect

### Your Email Address
We need this to create your account and send you important service notifications.

We do not sell your email address. We do not share it with advertisers. We use it only for account management and critical service updates.

### Your Password (Hashed)
We store a hashed version of your password. We never store your actual password.

A hash is a one-way encryption. We can verify your password is correct, but we cannot reverse the hash to see what your password is.

### Metadata You Provide
When you add filenames, dates, album names, locations, or notes, we store this information in plaintext.

We store metadata in plaintext so you can search your vault. If you do not want us to see a filename or note, do not include it.

We do not automatically extract metadata from your files. You control what metadata we see.

### Usage Data
We log when you log in, which features you use, and how much storage you consume. We use this to maintain the service and prevent abuse.

We do not track you across the web. We do not use analytics that share data with third parties. Our logging is internal only.

### Payment Information
We process payments through Stripe or crypto payment providers. We do not store your credit card numbers. Stripe handles that.

We store a record of your subscription status, plan type, and payment history. We need this to provide your service.

---

## What We Do Not Collect

We do not collect your browsing history. We do not track where you came from or where you go after leaving our site.

We do not use cookies for advertising or tracking. We use only essential cookies for session management.

We do not run analytics that phone home to third parties. No Google Analytics. No Facebook Pixel.

We do not scan your photos for faces, objects, or content. We have no idea what your photos contain.

We do not train AI models on your data. We cannot see your data to train anything.

---

## How We Use Your Information

We use your email to send account notifications. Password resets. Subscription expiration warnings. Security alerts.

We use your subscription status to enforce plan limits and grant access.

We use usage logs to monitor service health, prevent abuse, and troubleshoot issues.

We use metadata you provide to let you search your vault.

We do not use your information for advertising. We do not sell your information to anyone.

---

## Who We Share Your Information With

### Payment Processors
Stripe processes credit card payments. They see your billing information. They do not see your photos.

Crypto payment providers process cryptocurrency payments. They see your payment transaction. They do not see your photos.

### Storage Providers
We use S3-compatible object storage to store your encrypted files. Storage providers see encrypted blobs. They cannot decrypt them.

### Email Service
We use a transactional email service to send you emails. They see your email address and the emails we send you. They do not see your photos.

### Law Enforcement
If we receive a valid legal request, we will provide the information we have. This includes your email address, subscription status, and metadata.

We cannot provide your photos because they are encrypted. We cannot provide your encryption keys because we do not have them.

## Your Rights

You can export all your data anytime. Use the exports feature.

You can delete your account anytime. Go to settings and click delete account. Your data will be permanently removed within 30 days.

You can request a copy of what personal information we hold about you. Email us.

You can correct inaccurate information. Update your settings or email us.

You can opt out of non-essential emails. Click unsubscribe in any email we send.

---

## Data Retention

We keep your data as long as your account exists.

If you delete your account, we permanently delete your encrypted files and personal information within 30 days.

Backups containing your data are securely erased within 30 days of account deletion.

If your subscription expires and you do not renew, we keep your data in read-only mode for 90 days. This gives you time to export or renew. After 90 days, we may delete your account.

---

## Security

We use industry-standard security practices.

All data is encrypted in transit with TLS 1.2 or higher. All media is encrypted at rest with keys only you control.

We limit employee access to production systems. Employees cannot access your encrypted files even if they tried.

We run regular security audits. We patch vulnerabilities promptly.

If we experience a data breach, we will notify you within 72 hours. Because your files are encrypted, a data breach would expose encrypted blobs, not readable photos.

## International Users

BoosterAi.me is operated from the United States. Your data is stored on servers located in the United States or other regions depending on our storage provider.

By using BoosterAi.me, you consent to your data being transferred to and stored in the United States.

If you are in the European Union, you have additional rights under GDPR. Contact us to exercise these rights.
