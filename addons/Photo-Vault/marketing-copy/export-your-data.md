## What You Can Export

Everything. Your encrypted photos and videos. Your metadata. Your album structures. All of it.

All your encrypted media files in their original format. A manifest file listing every item with its metadata. Album information showing how your media is organized.

## How Exports Work

Log into your account. Go to the Exports page. Click "Create Export."

You can export your entire vault or select specific albums and date ranges. We process your request and create a ZIP file. This can take a few minutes depending on how many files you have.

When the export is ready, you receive a download link. The link is valid for 24 hours. Download the ZIP file to your computer.

You can create as many exports as you want. There is no limit. There is no fee.

## What Happens After Export

You have a ZIP file on your computer. It contains your encrypted files and a manifest.

The encrypted files are in standard formats. You can decrypt them using your password and the Web Crypto API. We provide documentation for this process.

Your data remains in BoosterAi.me after export. Exporting does not delete anything. You can continue using the service normally.

If you want to leave BoosterAi.me, export your data first. Then you can delete your account. Your exported files remain on your computer.

## No Data Hostage

We do not hold your data hostage when you stop paying.

If your subscription expires, your account becomes read-only. You can log in. You can view your files. You can create exports. You cannot upload new files.

You have 90 days after expiration to export your data. During this time, all your files remain accessible. After 90 days, we may delete your account to free up storage. We email you before this happens.

Even after deletion, if you have exported your data, you still have it. You own the files. They are on your computer.
