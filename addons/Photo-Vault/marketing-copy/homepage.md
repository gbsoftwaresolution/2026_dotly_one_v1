## What BoosterAi.me Does

We store your photos and videos after you encrypt them.

Your files are encrypted in your browser using keys only you control. We receive scrambled data. We cannot decrypt it. We cannot see your photos. We cannot hand them over to anyone, because we do not have access.

This is not a feature you turn on. It is how the system works.

---

## What We Do Not Do

We do not scan your photos.

We do not run facial recognition. We do not train AI models on your data. We do not profile you. We do not sell ads. We do not track you across the web.

We do not read your files. We cannot read your files.

---

## How It Works

You upload a photo. Your browser encrypts it before sending. We store the encrypted file. When you want to view it, we send the encrypted file back. Your browser decrypts it.

The encryption key never leaves your control. You can export all your data anytime. You can delete your account and everything we store about you will be removed.

## Trust & Transparency

BoosterAi.me is designed so you do not have to trust us with your photos.

We cannot see your files because they are encrypted before they reach our servers. This is not a promise. It is a technical constraint.

We store metadata like filenames, dates, and album names in plaintext so you can search your vault. If you do not want us to see this information, do not include it.

You can read our privacy policy and terms of service. They are written in plain language.

## Disclaimer

Zero-knowledge encryption means we cannot recover your files if you lose your password or encryption key. There is no account recovery. Back up your key. Write it down. Store it safely.

If you forget your password, your encrypted files are permanently inaccessible. We cannot help you. This is the cost of true privacy.
